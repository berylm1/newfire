## What Was Deployed

### 1. Zero-Trust Networking (zrok2 + OpenZiti)

Replaced traditional port-forwarding with a zero-trust overlay network. Services are now "dark" by default and only accessible through authenticated, encrypted tunnels.

**Stack (6 containers on Minisforum):**

| Service | Port | Purpose |
|---------|------|---------|
| Ziti Controller | :1280 | Identity and policy management |
| Ziti Router | :3022 | Encrypted data plane |
| zrok2 Controller | :18080 | Share management + admin console |
| zrok2 Frontend | :8080 | Public share proxy |
| PostgreSQL | internal | zrok2 database |
| RabbitMQ | internal | Real-time share mapping |

**What this means for clients:**
- No open ports on our network. Services are invisible to the internet.
- Every connection is end-to-end encrypted with cryptographic identity verification.
- We can give each client a unique, secure URL to access their agents.
- Adding new machines to the network takes one command (`zrok2 enable`).

**Verified working:**
- Grafana accessible via `http://ddoszysgxki7.share.100.79.80.119.sslip.io:8080`
- OpenHands accessible via `http://n4o3ccndzm1k.share.100.79.80.119.sslip.io:8080`
- Both tunneled through zero-trust overlay from their respective machines
